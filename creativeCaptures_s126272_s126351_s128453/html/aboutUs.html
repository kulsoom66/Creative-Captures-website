About us

Creative Captures will serve all the Omanis and the experts who are passionate about Photography.

What is our objectiv?

  1   Supporting and encouraging the talents of the photographers, by displaying their photos for sale on the site for customers.

  2   Providing photography tools to all photographers, as some people cannot buy these expensive tools.

  3   Ease of access for photographers, as the site displays photographers' accounts so that the customer can communicate with the photographer and then agree with him to carry out a photographic task.

  4   Improving the performance of the photographer, by allowing the customers to write their opinion about the picture, so the photographers can benefit from these comments to develop and improve their skill.
